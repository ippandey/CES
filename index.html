CIVIL ENGINEERING SOCIETY
OUR VISION
We encourage young minds to pursue their thirst for knowledge and curiosity in civil engineering by promoting holistic growth. We seek to stimulate collective growth among students through collaboration, ensure their holistic development, and provide experiential learning platforms in the form of competitions, activities, and meetings as part of our goal
OUR MISSION
Our aim is to promote teamwork, companionship and guidelines for professional and ethical behavior. It aims to raise knowledge among the students of the most recent trends and technology, making it easier for them to choose their career paths.
SVNIT
Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat popularly known as National Institute of Technology, Surat, NIT Surat or SVNIT, is an engineering institute of higher education established by the Parliament of India in 1961.It's mission is- "To be a globally accepted centre of excellence in technical education catalysing absorption, innovation, diffusion and transfer of high technologies resulting in enhanced quality for all the stake holders"
